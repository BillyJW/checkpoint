Why we're here

Never Drink Solo is committed to the ideal that no event should be celebrated by only one individual, and no event is too small to celebrate. Whether you're celebrating a birthday with friends, a graduation, with family, or simply celebrating getting through another day of work with your coworkers. Food and drink always tastes better with company.

How it works

Life only seems to move faster and faster as time goes. Keeping up with old friends, or making new friends after a fresh start in a new city isn't always easy. Never Drink Solo makes these things simpler so you can spend more time making new memories.


-Search for open gatherings near you, or narrow your search and look for gatherings geared towards: birthdays, holidays, game nights, etc.


-Make your own gathers with your own themes.


-Invite one or several groups of friends or create an open even where anyone can join.
